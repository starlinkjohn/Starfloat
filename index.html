Privacy Policy — StarfloaTik Hotspot

Effective Date: April 22, 2026
Last Updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how StarfloaTik Hotspot ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. The App is developed by Starfloat.

1. Information We Collect

StarfloaTik Hotspot is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers or any third parties.

The App operates as a local client that communicates exclusively with the MikroTik router device that you configure. All data exchange happens directly between your phone and your own router on your local network.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device, in the operating system's secure storage:

  • Router connection details (IP address, port)
  • Router login and password
  • Your preferences (biometric login, display settings)
  • Optional local history of actions you perform within the App (user creations, deletions, traffic limit changes, counter resets), retained for up to 90 days

This information never leaves your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing the App data or uninstalling the App.

3. Biometric Authentication

If you choose to enable biometric login (Face ID / Fingerprint), authentication is performed entirely by your device's operating system. The App never receives, processes, or stores your biometric data. We only receive a yes/no confirmation from the operating system.

4. Router Data

The App reads and modifies configuration data on your own MikroTik router (such as hotspot users, active sessions, interfaces, firewall rules). This data stays on your router and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet / Network state — to communicate with your MikroTik router over your local network.
  • Biometric / Fingerprint — only if you choose to enable biometric login.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a professional tool not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
